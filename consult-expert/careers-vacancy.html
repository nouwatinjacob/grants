Associate Consultant Intern

  • Project management
  • Amsterdam, Netherlands
Apply Now
Share

  • Requirements

The following are general qualifications and abilities required for the associate consultant intern position at Consult Care. Please check the local office page for any additional requirements.

  • Currently studying for a bachelor’s degree from a renowned educational institution.
  • A solid team player who’s also an independent thinker.
  • A robust analytical skill set, an endless curiosity and a penchant for thinking the impossible.
  • Deadline-driven, organized and able to multitask.
  • Strong verbal, written and presentation skills.
  • Fluency in English is mandatory, but please check the local office page for any additional requirements.
  • At the end of the summer, ACIs who have met performance expectations will receive an offer to return full­-time as an associate consultant once they have graduated from college or university.
